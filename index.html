What We Do

We are a group of international and domestic students who come together to create an environment for international students to feel comfortable and welcome on campus. We share cultures from around the world through various events that resemble holidays, festivals, or traditions. We even have study tables every monday from 6:15 - 7:15 in Bracken Library. Check out the calendar below for more. Join us to make the most of your college experience!
